Variance and bias in gene expression

We’ve established that CCS plays a role in survival across multiple datasets, and has something to do with anatomical location of the primary tumor. Let’s dive further into the whole genome expression data to find out more about this.

Quantify variance by location and CCS

Here, we analytically compare the variance explained by anatomical location and compare it to variance explained by CCS.
